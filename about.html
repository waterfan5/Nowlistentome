Now Listen To Me Geocacher

Waterfan5's Toolbox for Mystery Geocaches

The 'Now Listen to me' website is mostly written because it is useful for me, Arjen Bos, also known as Waterfan5 on geocaching.com website. I use it at home, and have written it as a website so I have the tools available when I am not at home.

Feel free to use it how you see fit.

ChatGPT and I wrote most of the tools that you can find on this website. Feel free to copy what you like, but I would love to hear where and how you are using it if you are copying a tool.

The tools are designed to make solving puzzles faster and quicker or to analyse information en masse to hopefully find the solution. Most tools show you a large amount of variations in the hope that the right solution is amongst them. This makes the tools different from what you see on other websites.

I update the tools frequently based on my input, but also sometimes break them, so be warned. However, feel free to contact me via this email address geocacher.waterfan5@gmail.com if you have questions, see a problem or have a suggestion.
